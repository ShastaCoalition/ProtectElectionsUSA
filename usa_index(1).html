🛡️ The 4 Federal Laws Congress Must Pass
Demand your representatives co-sponsor and vote for each one. These are the structural guardrails needed before the 2026 and 2028 elections.
1
Federal Defense 1
The 500-Foot Buffer
Prohibit the DOJ, FBI, ICE, and CBP from operating within 500 feet of any polling place, ballot counting location, or election office. Prevents ballot seizures and paramilitary voter intimidation.
✓ Threat stopped: Paramilitary/ICE at polls
2
Federal Defense 2
The Investigation Blackout
Mandate a freeze on all federal investigative steps involving elections beginning 30 days before Election Day and ending 7 days after the last member of the new Congress is seated.
✓ Threat stopped: October Surprises & DOJ weaponization
3
Federal Defense 3
Kill Forum Shopping
All election-related warrants must be sought ONLY by the Senate-confirmed U.S. Attorney for that specific district. Stops MAGA U.S. Attorneys executing warrants across state lines.
✓ Threat stopped: MAGA forum shopping
4
Federal Defense 4
Strip Absolute Immunity
Remove absolute and qualified immunity for officials who interfere with or deny a qualified voter's right to vote or have their ballot counted. Officials who refuse certification face personal liability.
✓ Threat stopped: Weaponized government workers
🎯 The Double Tap Strategy
Don't just call once. This two-step approach multiplies your impact. Skip email — politicians ignore it.
1
Step 1 — The Call
Speak Directly to a Staffer

Step 2 — The Social Multiplier
